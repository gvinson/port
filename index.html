PunkAid.org.

Web Design

PunkAid.org is a non-profit organization whose main goal is to simply help people in need. The overall theme that was desired was "grungy". The site needed to be designed so that it was easy to use due by visitors from other countries with lower bandwidths and who spoke a language other than English.

GingerZero.com.

Web Design

Ginger Zero is an alternative model based out of Chicago, IL. After the publication of this website, Ginger Zero has since become Chicago's go-to female alternative model.
The admin page allows the customer to upload new photos and photosets, update her news feed, add events to her events calendar, send newsletters to her subscribers, manage her subscribers and manage/respond to her shoot requests.
The website was built with PHP, MySQL, HTML, CSS and JavaScript. The design was created with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator CS5.
